Here are some key points you could consider including:
Location
This plant is in the best location for local farmers. The majority of feedstock taken by Ashton Keynes Green Energy will come from a 15km radius. Farmers who put the food on your plates are going to be the ones who benefit.
Farming Benefits
The plans support local farmers and the rural economy at a time when things have never been harder. Ashton Keynes Green Energy will use break crops and farm wastes from farmers to feed the plant and provide natural fertiliser in return to give farms the stability they need.
Homegrown Energy
The plans will produce green energy and support energy security. Biomethane produced will help our transition to net zero. This reduces our dependence on foreign gas supplies. The plant will also sequester CO2 which will address national shortages.
